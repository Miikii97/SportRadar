Breaking News:

Al Hilal maintain perfect ACL record with 2-1 away win

Al‑Hilal SFC (Saudi Arabia) continue their perfect start in the AFC Champions League Elite 2025/26, securing a 2-1 away victory against Al‑Gharafa SC of Qatar. The win gives them four wins out of four and puts them 2 points clear at the top of the West Region table.

Núñez and Hernández spark Al Hilal comeback to win 2-1

Al-Hilal also opened their campaign with a comeback 2-1 win over Al-Duhail SC of Qatar. Goals by Darwin Núñez and Theo Hernández secured the victory after going behind at half-time.

Nasaf beaten at home by Al Wahda after late header

PFC Nasaf of Uzbekistan are facing a tough run in their ACL campaign: they suffered a 1-2 home defeat to Al Wahda FC (UAE) thanks to an 89th-minute winner by Caio Canedo.
